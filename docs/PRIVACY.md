# Privacy

PoolPulse is privacy-first.

## What we collect
- Email for OTP authentication (contributors only)
- Pool, session type, lane type
- Timestamps (created_at, expires_at)

## What we do NOT collect
- No cameras / surveillance
- No precise location tracking
- No public display of user-level activity
- No names, photos, or personal profiles

## How data is shown
- Aggregated busyness by lane type (Fast/Medium/Slow)
- Ranges (Quiet/OK/Busy), not exact headcounts
- Check-ins auto-expire after 75 minutes
